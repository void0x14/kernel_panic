# Product Context

## Why this project exists
The project aims to provide a structured and immersive way to navigate time-series or chronological data. Instead of flat lists, users travel through a spatial "tunnel" of information.

## Problems it solves
- Information overload: By segmenting data into rings (time slices), users focus on one unit at a time.
- Boring data visualization: Traditional timelines are flat; the tunnel provides a "Future/Past" physical sensation.
- Contextual fragmentation: Synaptic connections (links) between visual entities maintain the relationship between data points.

## User Experience Goals
- **Vibe:** Clean, sci-fi, museum corridor. Orderly and professional.
- **Immersion:** Ray-like navigation (forward/backward) provides a sense of speed and direction.
- **Interactivity:** Clicking content should feel expansive (360-degree views or immersive modals).
- **Accessibility:** Users should be able to navigate regardless of their input method (Keyboard, Mouse).
